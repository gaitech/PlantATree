Deodar

Deodar tree is also known as Himalayan Cedar and Devadar in Hindi. The botanical name is Cedrus deodara and belongs to Pinaceae (Pine) family.

Tree Type: Evergreen Altitude: 1,500-3,200 meters lat, long: 1,500-3,200 meters Date: 12May, 2017
